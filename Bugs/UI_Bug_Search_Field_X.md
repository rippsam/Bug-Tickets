## [MTECH] Search field “x” covered by magnifying glass

---

### Description
On the [search results page](https://mtec.edu/search/), when you select the search field input to update or change you search, the ‘x’ to clear the search characters is overlapping with the magnifying glass.

---

### Replication Steps
1. On the [search results page](https://mtec.edu/search/), enter some text into the search field 
2. Move your cursor into the search field to make the ‘x’ populate 
3. Observe the 'x' and magnifying glass overlaping on the right side of the search field input

---

### Expected Behavior
The 'x' is to the left of the magnifying glass

---

### Actual Behavior
The 'x' is overlapping with the magnifying glass icon

---

### Environment
- **Browser / Device:** Chrome/Mac
- **Test Environment:** Production

---

### Customers Affected
MTECH Students
